# 2. Services
BoxingFitness currently provides:
- Bluetooth scanning, paired left/right smart boxing glove connection, connection status, and battery display;
- sensitivity settings, device control when training starts or ends, and Bluetooth hit-event recognition;
- Color Graffiti, Emotion Champ, Free Boxing, Rapid Fat Burn, Fat Burn Challenge, and Fat Burn Coach training modules;
- workout records, workout results, leaderboards, profile, language settings, and data sync services.
